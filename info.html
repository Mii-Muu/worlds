International and Domestic Voice Processes are customer service or telemarketing techniques that involve representatives communicating with customers over the phone.

International Voice Processes involve representatives communicating with customers from different countries, who may be speaking different languages. The aim of this process is to provide high-quality customer service, resolve any issues or complaints, and offer solutions that meet the customer's needs.
Domestic Voice Processes, on the other hand, involve representatives communicating with customers within the same country. This process is often used for sales, customer service, and support in industries such as telecommunications, retail, and finance.

Both International and Domestic Voice Processes require representatives to have excellent communication skills, active listening abilities, and the ability to work under pressure. They must also be able to handle a high volume of calls and have a customer-focused attitude.

In conclusion, International and Domestic Voice Processes play a crucial role in building customer relationships and maintaining customer satisfaction for businesses of all sizes.
